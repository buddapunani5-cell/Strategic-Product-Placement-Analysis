# Strategic Product Placement Analysis using Tableau

## Project Overview

Strategic Product Placement Analysis using Tableau is a retail analytics project that examines how product positioning, pricing strategies, promotional campaigns, customer demographics, competitor pricing, seasonal demand, and store foot traffic influence product sales performance.

The project transforms retail transaction data into interactive visualizations using Tableau, enabling businesses to identify high-performing products, evaluate placement strategies, and optimize merchandising decisions. The dashboard provides an intuitive interface for exploring sales trends and supports data-driven retail decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to determine the best product placement strategy for maximizing sales. Multiple factors such as shelf location, promotions, competitor pricing, customer demographics, and seasonal demand influence purchasing behavior.

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing retail data and presenting actionable insights through interactive dashboards and stories.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze average sales volume across different product categories.
- Evaluate the impact of product placement on sales performance.
- Compare product prices with competitor prices.
- Analyze consumer demographics and purchasing patterns.
- Measure the effectiveness of promotional campaigns.
- Study seasonal variations in product sales.
- Analyze customer foot traffic across different product positions.
- Develop interactive Tableau dashboards.
- Present insights using Tableau Story.
- Publish dashboards on Tableau Public.
- Integrate Tableau dashboards into a Flask web application.

---

## Worksheets

### Sheet 1 – Average Sales Volume vs Product Category

Compares the average sales volume across Clothing, Electronics, and Food categories.

### Sheet 2 – Consumer Demographics vs Sales Volume

Analyzes sales contribution from different consumer demographic groups.

### Sheet 3 – Competitor Price vs Price

Compares product prices with competitor prices to understand pricing competitiveness.

### Sheet 4 – Product Category vs Price

Visualizes average pricing across different product categories.

### Sheet 5 – Promotion of Product Category on Price and Sales Volume

Examines how promotional campaigns affect product pricing and sales volume.

### Sheet 6 – Average Sales Volume by Product Category by Product Position

Analyzes how different product positions influence average sales across categories.

### Sheet 7 – Average Sales Volume by Product Category by Season

Compares seasonal sales performance across product categories.

### Sheet 8 – Foot Traffic by Average Sales Volume

Studies the relationship between customer foot traffic and product sales.
